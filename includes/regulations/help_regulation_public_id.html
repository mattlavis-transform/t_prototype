Help with public-facing regulation identifiers

Public-facing regulation identifiers take the following forms, depending on the kind of regulation that is beigng set up.
Regulation type Public-facing identifier
Secondary legislation All secondary legislation is lodged on https://www.legislation.gov.uk and is searchable via its year and unique identifier. The public-facing identifier is written in the form

2019 No. 345
Tertiary legislation Public notices are created on www.gov.uk. Their public-facing identifiers are written in the form

Taxation Notice: 2019/007
